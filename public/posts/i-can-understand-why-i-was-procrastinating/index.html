Say ‘WHEN’ on a task will be done.

To do list - Calendar Transferring the author recommended. As we humanbeings are so obsessively managable by setting when we will do a specific task. He explained by an experiment with 2 groups of addicts. 2 groups both had to write resumes. One group is required to plan ‘When’ they will write resumes, and another group isn’t.
